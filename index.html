Decentralized Multi-Sig Escrow

Neutral. Anonymous. Protocol-Enforced.

We provide secure 2-of-3 Multi-signature Bitcoin escrow services for high-value transactions. In a world without trust, we enforce the agreement using cryptography. You do not need to trust us. You only need to trust the protocol.

Our Workflow

01

Agreement

Both parties define terms in a PGP-encrypted session.

02

Deposit

Buyer funds the unique 2-of-3 Multi-sig address.

03

Delivery

Seller verifies deposit and provides the product/service.

04

Release

Buyer confirms receipt. and we (2-of-3 signatures) release the fund to the buyer.

Dispute Resolution

If the agreement conditions are not met, either party may trigger a dispute. As the neutral third-party keyholder, we arbitrate based on predefined evidence protocols (PGP logs, verifiable delivery proof, etc).

Our verdict is final, enforced by the 2nd signature required to complete the transaction.

so we'll make sure both the buyer and seller goes home happy without any scams
